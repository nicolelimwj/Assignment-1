Wild Wild Wet

Wild Wild Wet is an outdoor water park located in the East of Singapore. Take the plunge with slides, rides and more here at Wild Wild Wet!

Wild Wild Wet, opened in 2004, is one of the top 10 water parks in Asia. After expanding in 2017, Wild Wild Wet now accommodates both thrill seekers with high speed water rides and families with young children with kid-friendly water slides and playgrounds. Spend a day out in the sun (and get wet!) here at Wild Wild Wet Singapore!


Slides

1. Kraken Racers

Race up to 3 of your friends as you slide down headfirst on the Kraken Racers.


2. Vortex

This slide is not for the faint of heart. Twist and turn as you slide down 4 stories high in Vortex!


3. The Water Works

Face the sun in this yellow whirpool of fun! Experience the serpentine water slide Water Works at Wild Wild Wet today.


4. Free Fall

Hold onto your heart as you experience the drop of a lifetime on Free Fall. The waterslide features a straight drop where you'll go at speeds up to 50feet per second!


5. Torpedo

Go at Torpedo speed as you plunge into this covered water slide. Feel the floor open up from under you and take the ride of your life down from 6 stories high!


Rides

1. Ular-Lah

Slides are not all there is at Wild Wild Wet, they've got rides too! Brave the rapids as you and up to 5 other friends experience Ular-lah together!


2. Royal Flush

Sit in a float up to 4 pax and feel what's it's like to be Royally Flushed - down a toilet!


3. Slide Up

Experience the water slide version of the famous Viking Ride and feel your heart drop as you go near vertical in Slide Up.


Attractions

1. Shiok River

Too tired out from all the fun on the rides? It's time to laze and float around the park in the Shiok River.


2. Tsunami

Battle the waves in Tsunami, Wild Wild Wet's very own wave pool! You can choose to float on the floats provided or just hang near the shore to get your feet wet.


3. Kid Zone

Bring your little ones along to the Kid Zone! Miniature slides and a wading pool for a safe and splashing good time!


4. Professor's Playground

Hang out at the Professor's Playground, designed like an experimental laboratory: Look out for slides, ladders, water cannons and fountains for your little ones to explore!


5. YIPPEE!

For toddlers who want to get used to the water, YIPPEE! is perfect with miniature slides and water features.
